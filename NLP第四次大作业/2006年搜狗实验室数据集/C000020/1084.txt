
　　Out of debt, out of danger.无债一身轻。
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Out of office, out of danger.无官一身轻。
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Out of sight , out of mind .眼不见,心不烦。
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Patience is the best remedy.忍耐是良药。
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Penny wise , pound foolish.贪小便宜吃大亏。
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Pleasure comes through toil .苦尽甘来。
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Pour water in to asieve.竹篮打水一场空。
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Practice makes perfect.熟能生巧。
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Praise is not pudding.恭维话不能当饭吃。
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Praise makes good men better , and bad men worse 
.好人越夸越好,坏人越夸越糟。
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Prefer loss to unjust gain.宁可吃亏,不贪便宜。
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Prevention is better than cure.预防胜于治疗。
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Pride goes before, and shame come safter.骄傲使人落后。
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Promise is debt .一诺千金。
